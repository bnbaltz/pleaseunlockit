QUESTION:

What is the pH of a 0.470 M solution of pyridine? Kb= 1.5 Ã— 10-9

ANSWER:

#

Pyridine dissociate as

C5H5N + H2O   C5H5NH+ + OH-

Kb = [C5H5NH+] [OH-] / [C5H5N]

pyridine is monobesic base therefore

[C5H5NH+] = [OH-] = x 

Kb = [x][x] /  [C5H5N]

Kb =[x]2 /  [C5H5N]

[x]2 = Kb   [C5H5N]

[x]2 = 1.5  10-9  0.470 = 7.05  10-10

[x] = 2.66  10-5

[C5H5NH+] = [OH-] = x  = 2.66  10-5

pOH = -log[OH-] = -log(2.66  10-5) = 4.58

pH = 14 - pOH = 14 - 4.58 = 9.42

pH of pyridine = 9.42
