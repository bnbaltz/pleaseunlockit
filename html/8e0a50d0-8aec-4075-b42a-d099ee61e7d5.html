Explanation:
Gymnosperms = Plants with naked seeds
Ginkos and Cyacdas contain swimming sperm.
Due to this reason, they are considered as lower gymnosperms (i.e. in bryophytes, and pteridophytes, the male gamete contains flagella. In angiosperms and other gymnosperms, male gametes do not contain locomotory organs).

 

2. Ginkgo is considered as the living fossil due to the following reasons.
It is one of the rare few dioecious plants.
i. Its leaves resemble the maidenhair fern
ii. It has no close relatives and appears to be the only plant species that belongs to the Permian period (270 million years). It is the only living species of Ginkgophyta. All the other species are extinct now.
