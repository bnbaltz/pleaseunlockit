QUESTION:

1.Which of the following correctly identifies the different phases of human development?

Select one:

a. Weeks 1 to 8 are called the embryonic development, and weeks 9 to 39 are called the fetal development.

b. Weeks 1 to 8 are called the fetal development, and weeks 9 to 39 are called the embryonic development.

c. The 1st trimester is called the embryonic development, the 2nd trimester is called the fetal development, and the 3rd trimester is when the baby grows to gain more weight.

d. The 1st trimester is fertilization and mitosis, and the 2nd and 3rd trimesters involve differentiation.

2.Which of the following structures develops into ONLY the fetus?

Select one:

a. Inner cell mass

b. Blastocyst

c. Trophoblast

d. Zygote

3.Which of the following is NOT a reason why this form of birth control is UNRELIABLE?

Select one:

a. Women always ovulate on the same day of their menstrual cycle every month.

b. Sperm can survive in the Fallopian tubes for 3 to 5 days.

c. It is difficult to predict exactly when in her cycle a woman ovulates.

d. Ovulation can be affected by stress or illness.

ANSWER:

Answer 1. The correct option is :-

C) The 1st trimester is called the embryonic development, the 2nd trimester is called the fetal development, and the 3rd trimester is when the baby grows to gain more weight.

In 1st trimester, there is fertilization, implantation and embryo development. In 2nd trimester, differentiation occurs and fetal development is there and in 3rd trimester fetal growth occurs.

Answer 2. The correct option is:-

B) Blastocyst

The zygote develops into a blastocyst, an embryo, then a fetus.

Answer 3. The unreliable reason is :-

A) Women always ovulate on the same day of their menstrual cycle every month.

The timings of the process of mensuration varies for every women and it may even vary every month.
