QUESTION:

The combustion of 5.00 grams of C2H6(g), at constant pressure releases 259 kJ of heat.
What is ?H for the reaction: 2C2H6(g) + 7O2(g) => 4CO2(g) + 6H2O(l)?

The answer the quiz gives is -3108 kJ How did they get this answer?
please show me the steps in your answer.

ANSWER:

2 C2H6(g) + 7 O2(g) => 4 CO2(g) + 6 H2O(l)

Moles of C2H6 = mass/molar mass of C2H6

= 5.00/30 = 0.16667 mol

Heat released by 0.16667 mol of C2H6 = 259 kJ

Heat released by 2 mol of C2H6 = 2/0.16667 x 259

= 3108 kJ

ΔH(reaction) = -Heat released by 2 mol of C2H6

= -3108 kJ

Note that ΔH is negative as reaction is exothermic and heat is released
