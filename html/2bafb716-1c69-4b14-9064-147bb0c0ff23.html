QUESTION:

LFM Corporation makes and sells a product called Product WZ. Each unit of Product WZ requires 3.4 hours of direct labor at the rate of $30.00 per direct labor-hour. Management would like you to prepare a Direct Labor Budget for June.

The company plans to sell 35,000 units of Product WZ in June. The finished goods inventories on June 1 and June 30 are budgeted to be 570 and 150 units, respectively. The budgeted direct labor cost per unit of Product WZ would be: (Do not round intermediate calculations.)

$3,527,160

$3,545,910

$1,042,500

$3,564,660

ANSWER:

 $3,527,160   
 Statement showing Labour Budget   
 Particulars   Amount 
 Opening Stock                 570.00
 Closing stock                 150.00
 Sales           35,000.00
 Production (Sales+CS-OS)(35000+150-570)           34,580.00
 Labour hours per unit                      3.40
 Total Hours reqd for prod'n         117,572.00
 Rate per Direct Labour hour                    30.00
 Budgeted Labour cost     3,527,160.00
