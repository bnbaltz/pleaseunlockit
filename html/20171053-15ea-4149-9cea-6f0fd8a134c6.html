QUESTION:

.          Situation Analysis (SWOT):

            A.        Historical Background of “Apple” Company

            B.        Industry Competitive Analysis

ANSWER:

 

Apple is a renowned premier brand recognized around the world. The brand "Apple" was created in 1976 by Steve Jobs and Steve Wozniak who initially sold personal computers through their company Apple Computers. In  the year 1977 they incorporated the company as Apple Inc. The first personal computer they introduced was a type writer kind of device which could be connected to a TV set for output. This was later referred as Apple I. After a moderate success of this device, they introduced an upgraded version known as Apple II. This had a gradual growth leading to huge success eventually. Apple II had a software app known as VisiCalc that could calculate on-screen in table form and became one of the main reasons for the success of this model. Apple III was introduced in due course with added hardware and functionalities and it continued the growth story of Apple products. 

The next success that Apple reaped was with the introduction of Macintosh series of computers, which had several innovations and features that form the core of present day computers. Thereafter Apple never looked back and went from strength to strength and introduced world famous products such as iPhone, iPad, Apple watch and personal computers among others. 

SWOT analysis of "Apple Inc."

Strengths

Its main strength is the high premium value its brand commands in the market. Besides, innovative products, features and software make Apple brand unbeatable by its contemporaries. 

Weakness

It has got limited number of products and variants as compared to competition which limits its coverage to a niche segment of buyers only. Secondly the high prices of the products also limits its expansion to the budget conscious consumers. 

Opportunities

Penetration in large consumer base countries in Asia; Expansion of iPhone and iPad tablets in more affordable variants and introduction of premier innovative products that will get instant recognition in the market.   

Threats

Competition from products originating from Asian countries that are cheap and technically advanced may lead to declining sales. Also the limited customization and adaptability of its iOS operating system as compared to rival Android based operating system may deter customers.
