Digital Image Processing

ANSWER:

Since height and width are the same, there is no need to calculate differently for both.

1) A line pair comprises a dark line and an adjacent light line. This means we need alteast 2 pixels, one dark and one light. for example, A resolution of 10 lines per millimeter means 5 dark lines alternating with 5 light lines, or 5 line pairs per mm. There can be 2048 lines possible. Hence 1024 line pairs are to be fitted in 5 cm = 50 mm space. Hence line pair per mm required = 1024/50 = 20.48 LP/mm.

2) DPI stands for Dots Per Inch. These dots can be pixels. Here, we need 2048 pixels in 2 inches. Which means resolution in dpi = 2048/2 = 1024 dpi.
