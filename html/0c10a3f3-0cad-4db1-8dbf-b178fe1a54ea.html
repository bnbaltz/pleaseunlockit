FICA Tax

Federal Insurance Contributions Act (FICA) tax is levied by employers. Each employer must pay the amount equal to the FICA taxes withheld from the employees’ annual gross earnings at the following rate:

• FICA – Social Security tax: 6.2% on the first $110,100 of each employee’s annual earnings.

• FICA – Medicare (medical benefits) tax: 1.45% on all earnings of employees.

The combine rate for Social security and Medicare tax is 7.65%.
