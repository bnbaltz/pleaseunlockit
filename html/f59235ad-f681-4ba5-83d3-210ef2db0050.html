ANSWER:


(a)

When buyers expect price of a good to fall in future then they reduce their current demand for the good.

So,

If buyers widely expect that the price of gold will fall soon in the future then they will decrease their demand for gold.

This decrease in demand for gold will shift the demand curve to the left.

Given the supply, this decrease in demand for gold will lead to fall in the price of gold.

So,

The equilibrium market price of gold will fall today.

(b)

When sellers expect price of gold to fall in future they tend to increase their current supply of gold.

So,

If sellers widely expect that the price of gold will fall soon in the future then they will increase their supply of gold.

This increase in supply of gold will shift the supply curve to the right.

Given the demand, this increase in supply of gold will lead to the fall in the price of gold.

So,

The equilibrium market price of gold will fall today.
