ANSWER:

In nuclear fission the nucleus disintegrates or splits into two or more smaller nuclei as a product. In the nuclear fusion process the sum of the number of neutrons and protons of reactants is equal to the sum of the number of neutrons and protons of the product. 

 

n + ²³⁵U → ²³⁶U → ¹³⁷Xe + ⁹⁷Sr + ​​​​​​ ​​​​​​2  n

For the above Nuclear fission reaction two neutrons are generated. Two neutrons are generated as the sum of all the mass number on the left hand side of fission reaction must be equal to the sum of all the mass number on the right hand side of the reaction.

 

Total Mass number of reactants = 236    ..... (1)

Total mass number of product = 137 + 97 + x     .....(2)

Equation (1) must be equal to equation to (2), therefore

236 = 137 +97 + x

So , x = 2
