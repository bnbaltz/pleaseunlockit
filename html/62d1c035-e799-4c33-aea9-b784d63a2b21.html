QUESTION:

How do intellectual revolutions transform societies?

ANSWER:

A revolution in moral and social philosophy which is as relevant and important as any other  revolution, which had an impact on the society and mindset of the people is known as intellectual revolution. History has witnessed many intellectual revolutions like freudian revolution, darwinian revolution, copernican revolution etc..During this period, people started to think in a more rational and logical way  of thinking rather thantraditional thinking.it mainly talk about reform and transformation.The communities are constantly moving toward change in ideas,customs and concepts.These revolutions shape the society and develop a goal to raise human value.Intellectual revolution hapens when the intellectuals are able to express, explain and produce a particular idea or information, to employ them effectively in the society.Intellectual revolution has to strive despite the obstacles in reforming the society or the objections when an idea is put forward.Intellectual revolutions awaken the society, move them away from superstitions an d beliefs. It enables critical and rational thinking among people.It helps in the determination of an indvidual, a continuous discovery of selfworth, determination .It engages people in striving toward equal oppoetunities.
