Crime control model:

Crime control model propagates the theory of strict implementation of criminal justice system in the country. It aims at consistently reducing crimes in the society by effective policing and prosecution. It compromises individual rights for the societal well being.

Contradiction in ‘discovery’ and ‘crime control model’:

The crime control model believes that the accused is guilty and therefore is taken into custody. Hence, the court proceeds with the case to prove the convict as guilty. On the contrary the discovery rules allow the defense to have access to the information available with the prosecution.

This information availed is used by the defense to counter the arguments in the court. This will increase the chance of a suspect being proven innocent by necessary inspection of the evidence. The court may pass the judgment in favor of the suspect that leads to his release. Thus, the motive of crime control model is not fulfilled.
