QUESTION:

In the last reporting period, Helena's Heavenly Fixture Company recorded 100,000 units sold for the first time in the history of the company. The price per unit was $89.99 and variable costs per unit at $36.39. Showing all work in the space provided, compute the contribution margin. Next, compute the fixed costs if the operating income is $4,020,000.

 

Compute the contribution margin.                                                                              

 

Compute the fixed costs if the operating income is $4,020,000                                             

ANSWER:

               
Computation of Contribution margin          
               
Sales (100000 units * $89.99)   $8,999,000        
Less : Variable costs (100000 units * $36.39) $3,639,000        
Contribution margin   $5,360,000        
               
Computation of Fixed costs            
               
Operating Income = Contribution margin - Fixed costs        
Hence Fixed costs = Contribution margin - Operating Income = $5360000 - $4020000 = $13,40,000
