QUESTION:

Determine the price of a $200,000 bond issue under each of the following independent assumptions:

                  Maturity            Interest paid            Stated Rate        Effective rate

1.               10 years              annually                      10%                    12%

2.               10 years              semiannually               10%                    12%

3.                20 years             semiannually               12%                    12%

Please help me solve with explanation. Thank you.

ANSWER:

1) 

Price of Bond = pv(rate,nper,pmt,fv)

rate =12%

nper  = 10 

pmt (Coupon Amount) = 10%*200000 = 20000

fv = 200000

Price of Bond = pv(12%,10,20000,200000)

Price of Bond = $ 177,399.11

2)

 

Price of Bond = pv(rate,nper,pmt,fv)

rate =12%*1/2 = 6%

nper  = 10 *2 = 20

pmt (Coupon Amount) = 10%*200000*1/2 = 10000

fv = 200000

Price of Bond = pv(6%,20,10000,200000)

Price of Bond = $ 177,060.16

3)

 

Price of Bond = pv(rate,nper,pmt,fv)

rate =12%*1/2 = 6%

nper  = 20 *2 = 40

pmt (Coupon Amount) = 12%*200000*1/2 = 12000

fv = 200000

Price of Bond = pv(6%,40,12000,200000)

Price of Bond = $ 200,000
