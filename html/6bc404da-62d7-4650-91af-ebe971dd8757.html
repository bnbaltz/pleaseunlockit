Goodwill

Goodwill is an intangible asset. It arises when one company purchases or acquires another company.

Recording of goodwill:

• Goodwill is the excess of cost of an acquired company over the sum of its net asset. Net asset is the difference between total asset and total liabilities.

• It is the value paid above the net worth of the company’s assets and liabilities Goodwill is shown in the asset side of the balance sheet under intangible asset section.

• Therefore, goodwill is only recorded, while acquiring from other company.
