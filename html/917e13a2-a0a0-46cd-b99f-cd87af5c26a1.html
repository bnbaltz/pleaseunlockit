QUESTION:

Consider the following figure.

(a) Find the equivalent resistance between points a and b in the figure. (R = 18.0 Ω)

 

(b) Calculate the current in each resistor if a potential difference of 60.0 V is applied between points a and b.
I (4.00 Ω) = _____ A
I (7.00 Ω) = ______ A
I (18.0 Ω) = _______A
I (9.00 Ω) = _______ A

ANSWER:

a) Req between points a and b =4+ {(7*18)/(7+18)}+9=4+5.04+9=18.04 Ω

b) To calculate the current through the resistors, we consider;

         i) Current through resistors in series is the same

         ii) Resistors in parallel have the same voltage drop

Current through I (4.00 Ω)=V/R=60/18.04=3.33 A

Equivalent resistance of the parallel resistors (7Ω and 18 Ω)=(7*18)/(7+18)=5.04 Ω

Voltage drop across the two resistors =3.33*5.04=16.78 V

I (7.00 Ω)=16.78/7=0.97 A

I (18.0 Ω)=16.78/18=0.93 A

I (9.00 Ω)=60/18.04=3.33 A
