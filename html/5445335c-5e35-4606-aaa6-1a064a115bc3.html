QUESTION:

Two springs, with force constants k1=125N/m and k2=245N/m , are connected in series.

When a mass m=0.75kg is attached to the springs, what is the amount of stretch, x ?

Express your answer to two significant figures and include appropriate units.

ANSWER:

f = kx

x = f/k

for spring 1 x1 = f/k1

for spring 2 x2 = f/k2

x = x1+x2

f/k = f/k1+f/k2

1/k = 1/k1+1/k2

k = k1k2/k1+k2

k = 125*245/370 = 82.8 N/m

x = f/k = mg/k = 0.75*9.8/82.8

x = 0.089 m = 8.9 cm
