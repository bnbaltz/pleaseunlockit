ANSWER:

Electric potential at point P due to charges at cornersis  V = ( K/ r )  [-4q+q+4q-q]
                                                                                        = 0
where  r = distance of point P from corner of thesquare
Electric potential due to remaining charges  V ' = ( K /a ) [ -2q +5q -5q -2q ]
                                                                      = ( K / a ) [ -4q ]
                                                                      = [ ( 1/ 4πε ) / d ] [ -4q ]
So, potential due to all charges  V " = V + V '
                                                     = [ ( 1/ 4πε ) / d ] [ -4q ]
Note : Potential is scalar
