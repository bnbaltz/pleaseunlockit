QUESTION:

Do you think Canada should return to backing up our dollar with a gold-based reserve system? Why/Why not? What would be some of the advantages and disadvantages?

ANSWER:

19 Proponents of the gold standard defend their viewpoint by arguing that gold can be stable in value and this will reduce risk of economic crises.  It will also restrict the power of the government, bring down the US trade deficit and present wars by limiting spending on defense. So this could be the advantages. However mobility of gold is less and hence it does not capture market fluctuations quickly. Gold cannot be create as it is a natural resource and is limited in supply, unlike money which can be printed to ease out economic unheavels.
