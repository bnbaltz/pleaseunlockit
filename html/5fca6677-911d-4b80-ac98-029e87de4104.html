QUESTION:

Use the values provided below to calculate the predicted isoelectric point, pI, value for aspartame.

 

ANSWER:

Looking at the structure of aspartame, we have aspartic acid side chain and NH3+ of aspartic acid.

For acidic amino acid that is the aspartic acid, forming aspartame we would use the pKa value of aspartic acid side chain COOH and of NH3+.

taking the average of the two = 3.65 + 9.60/2 = 6.63

So the PI for aspartame becomes,

6.63
