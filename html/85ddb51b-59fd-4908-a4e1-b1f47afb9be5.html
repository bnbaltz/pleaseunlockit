b) A 2-Form context free grammar is defined as:

     A→ α | β    where A is in V and α ,β∈ (V+T)* 

A grammar is context free if it has one Non-Terminal (V) on LHS of production and any combination of Terminals and Non-terminals on RHS. The above definition is in accordance with the standard context free grammar, and hence, being a Context free grammar, it generates the class of Context free languages.
