r = radius of the inkdrop = 15 x 10-6 m 

Volume of the inkdop is given as 

V = 4r3/3 = 4(3.14)(15 x 10-6)3/3 = 1.413 x 10-14 m3

 = density = 1000 kg/m3 

mass of the inkdrop is given as 

m = V = (1000) (1.413 x 10-14) = 1.413 x 10-11 m3 

L = length of the plates = 2 cm = 0.02 m 

v = speed of the drops in horizontal direction = 20 m/s 

t = time of travel

Since there is no acceleration along the horizontal direction, we have 

t = L/v = 0.02/20 = 0.001 sec 

y = vertical deflection = 0.30 mm = 0.30 x 10-3 m

a = acceleration 

vo = initial velocity along the vertical direction = 0 m/s 

Using the kinematics equation 

y = vo t + (0.5) a t2 

0.30 x 10-3 = (0) (0.001) + (0.5) a (0.001)2 

a = 600 m/s2 

q = charge on the drop 

E = magnitude of electric field between the plates = 8 x 104 N/C

acceleration of the drop due to electric field is given as 

a = qE/m

600 = (8 x 104)q/(1.413 x 10-11)

q = 1.06 x 10-13 C
