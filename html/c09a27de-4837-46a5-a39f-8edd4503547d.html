QUESTION:

Molecular biology 

ANSWER:

The CAG repeats are same at the 5' and 3' end of the gene, which makes it simple identify the number of repeats present using a PCR based on the amplicon. This will give an indication whether it early onset or late onset based on the size of the amplicon.

The forward primer will be 5' CAGCAGCAGCAGCAGCAGCAGCAG 3'

The reverse primer will be 5' CTGCTGCTGCTGCTGCTGCTGCTG 3'

The individuals who do not develop HD will typically have a amplicon size of 18 - 117 bps.

The individuals with adult onset HD will have a amplicon size of 120 - 165bps

The individuals with childhood onset will have amplicon size of more than 200 bps.
