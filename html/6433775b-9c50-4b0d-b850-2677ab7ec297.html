QUESTION:

A key lime pie in a 10.00 inch diameter plate is placed upon a rotating tray. Then, the tray is rotated such that the rim of the pie plate moves through a distance of 108 inches. Express the angular distance that the pie plate has moved through in revolutions, radians, and degrees.

(________) revolutions

(________)radians

(________)degree

If the pie is cut into 9 equal slices, express the angular size of one slice in radians, as a fraction of pie?

revolutions = 108/31.4 = 3.439 revolutions

radians = 3.439 *2 = 21.6 radians

degrees = 21.6 * 180/ = 1238.216 degrees              ( radians =180 degrees)
