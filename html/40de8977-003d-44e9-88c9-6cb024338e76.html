Book Value of the Equipment as on the date of sale

Book Value of the Equipment as on the date of sale = Original cost of the Equipment – Accumulated Depreciation Expense

= $75,000 - $50,000

= $25,000

 

Gain on sale of the Equipment

Gain on sale of the Equipment = Sale Proceeds – Book Value of the Equipment

= $40,000 - $25,000

= $15,000 Gain

 

Here, the Sale Proceeds ($40,000) is greater than the Book Value of the Equipment ($25,000), therefore the Gain on sale of Equipment will be $15,000
