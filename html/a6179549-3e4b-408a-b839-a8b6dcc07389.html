QUESTION:

Which one of the following changes will necessarily increase the capacitance of a capacitor? Question 10 options: a) increasing the potential difference between the plates b) decreasing the charge on the plates c) placing a dielectric between the plates d) decreasing the potential difference between the plates e) increasing the charge on the plates

ANSWER:

Capacitance of a Capacitor depends on the dimensions of the conductor, and the medium.

Therefore only placing a dielectric between the plates can effect the Capacitance.

Answer (C)
