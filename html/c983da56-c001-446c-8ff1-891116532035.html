D-H Parameter for each Link

The fundametal of serial- link robot kinematics and theDenavit-Hartenberg.Each link is represented by two parameters:the link length (a),and the link twist (∝),which define the relative location of two parameters:the link offset(d),which is the distance from one link to the next along the axis of the joint,and the joint angle(θ),which is the rotationof one link with respect to the next about the joint axis.For a revolute joint(θi)is the joint variable and di is constant,while for a prismatic joint di is a variable and θi is the constant.The link length and link twist are constant.

Uing attched frames the four parameters that locate one frame relative to another are define as:

  1. θi joint angle is the angle between the xi-1 and xi axes about the zi-1 axis;
  2. d1 (link offset) is the distance from the orgin of frame {i-1} to the xi axis along the zi-1 axis;
  3. a1 (link length)is the distance between the zi-1 and z1 axes along the x1 axis ; for the intersecting axes is parallel to zi-1 × zi;
  4. ∝i(link twist)is the angle between the zi-1 and zi axes about the xi axis.
