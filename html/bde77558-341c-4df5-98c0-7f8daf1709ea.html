Given that 

  • N1, N2, N3, N4 be number of basketball goals, encyclopaedia sets, field trips, computer stations respectively.
  • P1, P2 be Priority 1 and Priority 2 respectively

Yi- :

Represents how much we must add to equal the goal i.e. the slack (the negative sign represent that we have fallen short of the goal)

Yi+ :

Represents how much we must subtract to equal the goal i.e. the excess (the positive sign represent that we have exceeded the goal)

  • Where i = 1, 2, 3, 4

Objective Function

  • Minimize P1*Y1- + P1*Y2+ + P2*Y3- + P2*Y4- + P2*Y5+

         S.t.

  • 400*N1 + 750*N2 + 300*N3 + 800*N4 + Y1- - Y1+ = 20,000                                                                         (GOAL 1: here we will minimize Y1- as we don't want to under spend)
  • 2*N1 + 3*N3 + 0.5*N4 + Y2- - Y2+ = 50                                                                                                                      (GOAL 2: here we will minimize Y2+ as we don't want to over spend volunteer time)

  • N4 + Y3- - Y3+ = 20                                                                                                                                                  (GOAL 3: here we will minimize Y3- as we don't want to under spend the request)
