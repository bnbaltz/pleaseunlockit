QUESTION:

Put yes or no in front of each of the following statements:
1- An inventory is the inventory of assets and liabilities in terms of quantity and peak
2-The reducing premium represents a percentage of the net value of the fixed asset
3- The imposition of independence of the accounting unit states that the project has an independent legal personality over the personality of the owner
4- One of the objectives of the inventory is to determine the real value of the liabilities and assets in the project

5- Expenses paid in advance from other credit balances that appear on the right side of the balance sheet

6- The principle of prudence and caution stipulates that one must be careful not to record potential expenses, other than potential revenues

ANSWER:

1)

No - Inventory is a Current assets and is shown under the Current Assets in the Balance Sheet. It does not represent a liability.

 

2)

Yes - The Reducing Premium is the Depriciation of an Asset and it is charged on the Net Value of the Asset.

 

3)

No - Independence means the accountant will not work under any influence. He will do his job as per the accounting guidlines and his fine judgement.

 

4)

No - The Value of the Inventory represents the real value of the asset because inventory is a current asset not a liability.

 

5)

No - Expenses paid in advance is paid by cash which is appear in the left side and has a debit balance.

 

6)

No - Prudence means an estimation is made as per the best judgement. The statement given above is done as per the Concept of Conservatism.
