Material Quantity variance: This variance indicates whether the actual quantity used was less or more than the standard quantity allowed for the actual output. This difference is multiplied by the standard price per unit of material because quantities cannot be entered into the accounting records.

Material Mix Variance : Material mix variance measures the effect of substituting a nonstandard mix of material during the production process.

Material Yield Variance : The material yield variance measures the difference between the actual total quantity of input and the standard total quantity allowed based on output; this difference reflects standard mix and standard prices.
