Correct Answer: TBI patients would be about 25% faster than ADE patients

Note: Even though it's said that baseline reaction time 0.25 can be ignored, we have to consider that also while calculating, to get the correct answer.

TBI: 

To brain delay: 2 seconds

Reaction delay: 0.25 seconds

Total: 2.25 seconds

ADE:

To brain delay: 1.5 seconds

Reaction delay: 1.5 seconds

Total: 3 seconds

To find out how much faster=

3 - 2.25 = 0.75 seconds

Percentage =

(0.75/3) x 100 = 25%
