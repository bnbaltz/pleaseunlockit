Answer:-

Brazil is the nation that have a large range of black populace. In Brazil, around half of one hundred and sixty million populace is from the mixed-race of African origin. In history, Brazilian Blacks were poorer than Brazilians of European regions, therefore, organizations were less targeting the market segment of black Brazilians. However, with the economic progression of Brazil and its black populace, a number of companies’ has commenced to invest in the Brazilian marketplace. (Hill, 2011)

Unilever has launched a number of skin care cosmetics and products for the black people of Brazil. The strategy of company is to realize the needs and desires of its target markets so that a product to fulfill their demand can be launched in that market. However, in Brazil, the company may face the racial dynamic matters. (Hill, 2011)

 Furthermore, this strategy of Unilever is dissimilar from its strategies in other nations. The global trade strategy of Unilever is based on USLP (Unilever Sustainable Living Plan), which targets on commencing the equal products or brands for its entire target marketplaces so that equal benefits to its entire stakeholders can be provided. (Bartlett, 2015)

This signifies that the overall business strategy of Unilever is to integrate all in one. Company chases the strategy of being global and sustainable. The overall trade strategy of Unilever is to unite all its efforts toward consumer insight so that equal brands to the entire consumers can be provided. This strategy of Unilever targets to make it a global brand. Unilever is putting efforts to compete with its opponents with the help of its sustainability strategy. (Bartlett, 2015)
