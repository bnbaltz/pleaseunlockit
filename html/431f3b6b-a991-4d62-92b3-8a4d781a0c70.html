Equilibrium occurs where QD of labor = QS of labor. This is true for a wage rate of $60. Hence equilibrium wage rate is $60 per hour and quantity of labor employed is 125 hours

Potential GDP, at 125 hours of labor (full employment) is $8125 and labor productivity = GDP/labor 

= 8125/125 = $65 per hour.
