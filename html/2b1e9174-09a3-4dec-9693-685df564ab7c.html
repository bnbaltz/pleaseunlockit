QUESTION:

Calculate the energy released during the Î±-decay of 230Th. The following atomic masses may be of use:
m(230Th) = 230.0331266 u,
m(228Th) = 228.0287313 u,

m(228Ra) = 228.0310641 u 

 

m(226Ra) = 226.0254026 u.

m(4He) = 4.00260325 u 

Please write the process, Thanks

ANSWER:

230Th-----> 4He + 226Ra

energy released = mass defect *C2  

where mass defect = M(Th) -[M(He) + M(Ra)] = 230.0331266 -[226.0254026+4.00260325] = 0.00512075 u 

C= speed of light 

u*C2 = 931.5Mev 

energy released  = 0.00512075*931.5 = 4.77Mev
