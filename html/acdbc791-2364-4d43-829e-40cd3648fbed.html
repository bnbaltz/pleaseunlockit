QUESTION:

At any instant the horizontal position of a weather balloon isdefined by x=8t m, where t is time in seconds.
 
If the equation of the path is y=x^2/10, determine
 
a) The magnitude and direction of the velocity at t=2seconds.
 
b) The magnitude and direction of the acceleration when t=2seconds.

ANSWER:

(a) x = 8.t
     y = 0.1.x2
Differentiating, vx = dx/dt = 8.0 m/s
    dy/t = vy = 0.1*2xdx/dt = 0.2x.vx
                  = 0.2*8*2x8 = 25.6 m/s
 
Hence, v = √(vx 2 +vy2) =  √(82 +25.62) = 26.8m/s
 
Direction of v:  Tan θ =vy/vx = 25.6/8 = 3.2 
                            θ  = 72.6o   with the xaxis
 
(b) Acceleration, a, at t = 2.0 s
Differentiating again,  ax = 0
                            ay = dvy/dt =d(0.2x.vx)/dt = 0.2.dx/dt.vx + 0
                                = 0.2.vx2 = 0.2* 8*8 = 12.8m/s2
Total acceleration,
                              a = 12.8m/s2 along the y direction.
