QUESTION:

Discuss a business model analysis of Amazon Inc. The analysis should include these metrics. Value proposition and relationships, Channels, Revenue streams, Key resources and Partners, Cost Structure.

ANSWER:

Amazon is one of the largest online retail store across the globe. The wide range of products within a single platform attract the customers. The proper and timely delivery of products help them to retain their customers.

Value Proportion and Relationship

Low Price

Wide range of products

Fast and Timely delivery of products

Easy Return of goods

Channel

www.amazon.com its website is the channel platform and also they have app that helps for easily access  their web store.

Revenue Stream

Amazons main revenue is through Prime Members subscription charges. They also get a particular percentage as commission from the seller.

Key Resource and Partners

The key resource of Amazon are their storage unit or ware houses center. Other resource is their updated and easily accessible software system.

Sellers are their main Partner; they are shipping the products within the time limits. Other partners are authors and publishers.

Cost Structure

The cost structure of amazon is low or economical. Amazons cost structure pattern helps them to increase their revenue.
