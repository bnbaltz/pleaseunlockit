QUESTION:

1.  Write a mechanism for the nitration of benzoic acid in the ortho and para positions (circle the bad resonance contributor).
2. Draw the energy diagram for the nitration of benzoic acid.

ANSWER:

Benzoic acid contains a carboxylic group which is strong deactivating group. Therefore, the nitration at ortho and para position is very difficult. In the resonance structure, there is destabilization of charge at aromatic carbon containing carboxylic group. It doesn't occur when the attack is at meta position. Thus,the energy required for formation of transition state at meta attack is less as compared to ortho and para attack as shown in energy profile diagram
