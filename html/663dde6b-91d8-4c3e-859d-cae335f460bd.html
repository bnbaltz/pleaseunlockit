QUESTION:

Three parallel plate capacitors, each having a capacitanceof 1.0 μF are connected in parallel.

The potential difference across the combination is 100 V.What is the charge on any one of the

capacitors.

ANSWER:


here each capacitor have voltage drop of 100 V
so charge on each capaciotor = Q = C * V = 1 μ F * 100 =100 μC
