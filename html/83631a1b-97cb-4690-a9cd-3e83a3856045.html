QUESTION:

Determine which structure is indicated in these two photos of the cross-section through an earthworm's head:

1.

A) ventral nerve cord

B) lumen

C) brain

D) pharynx

E) coelom

2.

A) genital sac

B) brain

C) nephridium

D) gizzard

E) pharynx

ANSWER:

Answer:

(1) The answer is  (C) Brain

* It's not ventral nerve cord because what is marked here is dorsal side.

* It's not lumen because the marked point is not in Pharynx region or even lumen is not seen in head region.

* It's not even coelom and Pharynx

So the final answer is "BRAIN" where cerebral ganglion presents.

 

(2) The answer is (E) Pharynx

The options genital sac, nephridium, gizzard can't be in head region andso they are eliminated.

So the final answer is "PHARYNX" which continues as gizzard and intestine.
