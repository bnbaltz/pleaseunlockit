Total 4 digit numbers less than 4000 that can be formed with digits 0-5 is computed as: 
_ _ _ _ 

There are 3 ways to fill the first digit and then 6 ways to fill rest of them: 3*6*6*6 = 648
We need to add that extra number 4000 here. Therefore there are total 649 numbers less than or equal to 4000 that can be made with digits 0-5

Also total such numbers is computed as: 5*6*6*6 = 1080

Therefore the required probability here is computed as: 
= 649 / 1080

= 0.6009 

Therefore 0.6009 is the required probability here.
