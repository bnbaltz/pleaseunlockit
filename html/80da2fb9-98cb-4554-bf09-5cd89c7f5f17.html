QUESTION:

Your car was in a small accident and you took it to Able Autobody first thing on a Monday morning. Able services cars with rate λ =1/2 /hour,

i.e... P(Current Car Finished in [t,t+τ])≈τ/2 for small τ, and works 8 hours a day (not including lunch).

You are third in line including the car being worked on when the shop opens.

Find the probability that your car will not be fixed that day.

ANSWER:

This process is poission process.

Given that ,λ =1/2 /hour,

Since poission probability distribution P(X=k)= (λ k*exp(-λ)) / k!

λ= mean value=1/2

exp= exponantial

k= random value=3 hour

k!= factorial of k.

Thus,

P(Current Car Finished in X=3) = (1/2 3 *exp(-3)) / 3!  =0.001

P(Current Car not Finished in X=3) = 1-0.001 = 0.999
