QUESTION:

A package slides down a ramp 4 meters high , and at the bottom hits a rough patch with friction (u= 0.12). How far will it slide, before it comes to a stop?

ANSWER:

Usimg conservation of energy 

Workdone by friction = change in K. E

u mg d = mgh

d= h/u = 4/0.12

d= 33.33 m
