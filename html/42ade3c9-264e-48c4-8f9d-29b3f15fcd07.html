Synopsis:

A Company started providing membership program. The program’s objective is to target parents, relatives such as grandparents, aunts, uncles, and caretakers who frequently purchase products such as baby wipes and diapers for babies and young infants.

1.

The following are the benefits of A. Mom’s membership program from consumer perspective:

• Members will get additional discounts on every purchase they make.

• Home delivery facilities provided by the organization will benefit the consumers in saving their time.

• Free shipping of products.

• Members will obtain special discounts on regular products purchased.

• Membership cost is very cheap.

The following are the disadvantages of A. Mom’s membership program from consumer perspective:

• Consumers have to wait for a long period if the product is not available in stock.

• A Company performs internet based business; consumers might have connectivity problems.

• Touch and feel of the product will be absent.

2.

The following are the advantages of A. Mom’s membership program from company perspective:

• Company will have more brand loyal customers.

• A Company has a website which shares wide variety of products with the consumers.

• By rendering internet based business, company covers huge number of consumers.

• Building and employee cost are very low.

The following are the disadvantages of A. Mom’s membership program from company perspective:

• Packing charges will be high.

• Company will not be able to provide clear product information in the website.

• Transportation cost will be high.
