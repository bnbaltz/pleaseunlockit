REA Model for K Cannon, Inc.

(a)

Preparation of the REA model of the purchase cash payments process:

The main activities in the company are requesting for raw materials when purchases are required which is done by purchase clerk, receive the requested raw materials by the receiving clerk and make the payment to the supplier. There are many accounting procedures done which is not required to be shown in a REA model unlike analytical flowchart.

The Resources are Inventory and Cash Events are Request Material, Receive Material and Disburse Cash Agents are Purchasing Clerk, Receiving Clerk, Cash Disbursement Clerk and Supplier.

(b)

Cardinalities for associations:

• The Cardinality is almost same as the explanation given in the solution 7. There is only one difference compared to the previous solution that the association between Receive Material and Disburse Cash is M, 1:1, 1. They have not mentioned that suppliers are giving materials on credit, hence for each Receive Material, there has to be one Disburse Cash event and cannot have 0 Disburse Cash.

• The association between the events and the agents here is that, for each event, there has to be only one agent and each agent can perform 0 or many of those events. So, all the cardinalities here are M, 0:1, 1
