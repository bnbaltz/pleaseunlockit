QUESTION:

A capacitor is initially charged to 3 V. It is then connected to a 6 V battery. What is the ratio of the final to the initial energy stored in the capacitor?

ANSWER:

The energy U of a capacitor is given by.

U = 1/2 CV^2

capacitance of capacitor is same in both activity.

So C is constant.

Hence U is directly proportional to V^2.

Hence ratio of final energy to initial energy will be.

U2/U1 = [V2/V1]^2

= [(6+3)/3]^2

= 9 {answer}
