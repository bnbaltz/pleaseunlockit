QUESTION:

El Tapitio purchased restaurant furniture on September 1, 2018, for $43,000. Residual value at the end of an estimated 10-year service life is expected to be $5,800.

 

Calculate depreciation expense for 2018 and 2019, using the straight-line method, and assuming a December 31 year-end.
  

ANSWER:

Annual depreciation = (Purchase cost - Residual value) / Service life = $(43,000 - 5,800) / 10 = $37,200 / 10

= $3,720

In 2018, furniture was depreciated for 4 months (1 Sept to 31 Dec). Assuming depreciation can be charged partially,

Depreciation expense, 2018 = $3,720 x (4/12) = $1,240

Depreciation expense, 2019 = $3,720
