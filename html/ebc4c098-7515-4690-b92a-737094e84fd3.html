ANSWER:

the electric potential energy of the system when the chargesare separated by a distance R is
U = V * q
V = (1/4πεo) * (q/R)
or U = (1/4πεo) * (q/R) * q =(1/4πεo) * (q^2/R)
the electric potetial energy when the distance between thecharges is reduced to R/2 is
U1 = V1 * q
V1 =  (1/4πεo) * (q/R/2) = 2 *(1/4πεo) * (q/R)
or U1 = 2 * (1/4πεo) * (q/R) * q = 2 *(1/4πεo) * (q^2/R) = 2 * U
therefore the electric potential eergy of the system isdoubled when the distance between the charges is (R/2).
