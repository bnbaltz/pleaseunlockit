QUESTION:

Percy's Wholesale Supply has earnings before interest and taxes of $121,000. Both the book and the market value of debt is $190,000. The unlevered cost of equity is 14.7 percent while the pretax cost of debt is 8.6 percent. The tax rate is 35 percent. What is the firm's weighted average cost of capital?
11.94 percent
14.01 percent
13.07 percent
12.65 percent
14.37 percent

ANSWER:

Answer is 13.07%

Value of Unlevered Firm = EBIT * (1 - tax) / Unlevered Cost of Equity
Value of Unlevered Firm = $121,000 * (1 - 0.35) / 0.1470
Value of Unlevered Firm = $535,034

Value of Levered Firm = Value of Unlevered Firm + tax * Value of Debt
Value of Levered Firm = $535,034 + 0.35 * $190,000
Value of Levered Firm = $601,534

Value of Equity = Value of Levered Firm - Value of Debt
Value of Equity = $601,534 - $190,000
Value of Equity = $411,534

Weight of Debt = Value of Debt / Value of Levered Firm
Weight of Debt = $190,000 / $601,534
Weight of Debt = 0.3159

Weight of Equity = 1 - Weight of Debt
Weight of Equity = 1 - 0.3159
Weight of Equity = 0.6841

Debt-Equity Ratio = Weight of Debt / Weight of Equity
Debt-Equity Ratio = 0.3159 / 0.6841
Debt-Equity Ratio = 0.4618

Levered Cost of Equity = Unlevered Cost of Equity + (Unlevered Cost of Equity - Cost of Debt) * (1 - tax) * (Debt-Equity Ratio)
Levered Cost of Equity = 0.1470 + (0.1470 - 0.0860) * (1 - 0.35) * 0.4618
Levered Cost of Equity = 0.1470 + 0.0183
Levered Cost of Equity = 0.1653 or 16.53%

WACC = Weight of Debt * Cost of Debt * (1 - tax) + Weight of Equity * Cost of Equity
WACC = 0.3159 * 8.60% * (1 - 0.35) + 0.6841 * 16.53%
WACC = 1.76% + 11.31%
WACC = 13.07%
