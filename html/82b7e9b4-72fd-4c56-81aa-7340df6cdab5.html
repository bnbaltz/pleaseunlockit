2) Yes, this is a neutral technical change. 

 

Explanation

Neutral technical change means any technical change which is not specific to increasing productivity of any particular factor. Here, a 30% increase in production is neither due to the increase in productivity of labour nor due to an increase in productivity of capital. It is completely exogenous to the production function. In simple words, it neutrally increases the productivity of the entire production process.
