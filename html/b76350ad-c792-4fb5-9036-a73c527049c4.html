We know monopoly maximises profit when marginal revenue=marginal cost and price>marginal cost 

1. Since P>AC, firm is having profit but firm is not maximising because MR<MC. Thus firm should reduce output to maximise output

2.Firm should reduce output to maximise profit in short run until MR becomes equal to marginal cost 

3.Firm should continue production at this point only because firm is producing at a point where MR=MC. I.e. maximising profit.

4. Firm should increase production, in order to maximise profit because When production increases, marginal revenue will decrease and MC may increase. Profit is maximised when MR=MC.
