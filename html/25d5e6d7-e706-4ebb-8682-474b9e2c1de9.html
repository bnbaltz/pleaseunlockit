QUESTION:


The freezing point of a solution of 1.048 g of an unknown nonelectrolyte dissolved in 36.21 g of benzene is 1.39°C. Pure benzene freezes at 5.48°C and its Kf value is 5.12°C/m. What is the molecular weight of the compound?


59.2 g/mol

54.0 g/mol

61.4 g/mol

42.4 g/mol

36.3 g/mol

ANSWER:

delta T = 5.48 - 1.39=4.09

4.09 = m x 5.12

m =0.799 = moles solute / 0.03621 Kg
moles solute =0.0289
MM = 1.048 g/ 0.0289=36.2 g/mol
