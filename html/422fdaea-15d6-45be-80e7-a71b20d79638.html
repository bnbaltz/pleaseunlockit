Three 15-立 and two 25-立 light bulbs and a 24 V battery are connected in a series circuit. What is the current that passes through each bulb?
 

 
1)  0.18 A
 
2)  0.25 A
 
3)  0.51 A
 
4)  0.74 A
 
5) 

The current will be 1.6 A in the 15-立 bulbs and 0.96 A in the 25-立 bulbs.

ANSWER:

when resistance are in series ,net resistance=(3*15)+(2*25)=95 ohm

current=voltage/net resistance=24/95=0.25 A
