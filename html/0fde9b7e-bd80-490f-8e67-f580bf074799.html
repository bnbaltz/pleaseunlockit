The calculation is as below:

Present value = p = 25,000

Future value = F = 29,000

Rate of interest = r =?

Number of years = n = 4

Number of compounding months in a year = m = 12

Hence, by the formula,

F = P (1 + r/m) ^ (n × m)

Or, 29,000 = 25,000 (1 + r/12) ^ (4 × 12)

Or, 29,000 / 25,000 = (1 + r/12) ^ 48

Or, 1.16 = (1 + r/12) ^ 48

Or, 1.16 ^ (1/48) = (1 + r/12) ^ (48/48)

Or, 1.16 ^ 0.020833333 = 1 + r/12

Or, 1.003096 – 1 = r/12

Or, 0.003096 × 12 = r

Or, r = 0.037152

Or, r = 0.0372 (rounded)

This is to be multiplied by 100 in order to get it in percentage form; (0.0372 × 100 =) 3.72%.
