QUESTION:

A 10kg picture is held in place by two wires, one hanging at 50degto the left of the vertical and the other at 45deg to the right ofthe vertical. What is the tension in the second wire?

ANSWER:

Here is a diagram

Since the picture is in equilibrium, the sum of forces in both thex and y direction will =0
So let's do the x-direction first
0=-T1sin50+T2sin45
T1=T2sin45/sin50

Now do the y-direction
0=T1cos50+T2cos45-mg
mg=T1cos50+T2cos45

now plug in the value for T1
mg=T2sin45/sin50*cos50+T2cos45
10*9.81=0.593T2+0.707T2
98.1=1.30T2
T2=75.5 N

and now knowing that information, you could easily plug it back into find T1
