QUESTION:

Which of the following associations is correct:

Question 1 options:

 

midbrain : respiratory regulation

 

cerebellum : ability to ride a bicycle without really "thinking" about it

 

pons : control of heart rate and vasoconstriction/vasodilation of the vasculature

 

basal nuclei : enhance muscle tone

 

pineal gland : homeostatic integrator (control center)

ANSWER:

Basal nuclei : enhance muscle tone

pineal gland : homeostatic integrator (control center)

             These are the correct answers . The basal nuclei stimulates the muacle tone and the pineal gland have the capability to control homeostasis.
