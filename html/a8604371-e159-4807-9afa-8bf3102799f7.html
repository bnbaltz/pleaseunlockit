QUESTION:

A metal sphere is filled with ammonia as part of a refrigeration device. If the radius of the sphere is 0.5 m, and the density of ammonia is 0.86 kg/m3, what is the mass of the ammonia if it fills up half of the sphere?

ANSWER:

Volume of sphere = 4/3 * pi * r^3 = 0.523 m^3 


Half of sphere = V/2 


Density = 0.86 kg/m^3 


Mass = density * volume = 0.86 *0.523/2


= 0.225 kg
