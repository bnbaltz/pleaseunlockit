QUESTION:

A 0.60-kg block attached to a spring with force constant 130 N/m is free to move on a frictionless, horizontal surface. The block is released from rest after the spring is stretched 0.13m. At that instant, find the force on the block and its acceleration.

ANSWER:

     Givne that the mass of block is m =0.60 kg
     Force constant is K = 130N/m
     The streched distance is x =0.13 m
 ----------------------------------------------------------------------
      spring Force F= kx    
                             = 130 N/m*0.13m
                              =16.9 N
     Then the accelaration is a = F/m
                                            =16.9 N / 0.60 kg
                                            =28.16m/s2
