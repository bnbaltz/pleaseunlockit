QUESTION:

Give a detail description of amazon key resources.

ANSWER:

Amazon is a web based company which provides platform where manufactures of the product interact with customers and sell their product online. In online shopping, scope of products is unlimited from books to toys and DVDs to electronic products. Based on its business model, the key resources of Amazon are followings -

  • Physical resources like warehouses and film studios as the physical goods are stored in its warehouses and film studies are used to produce digital content
  • Technological infrastructure as Amazon has a well-designed infrastructure for effective managing its business functions and the interactions between different components of the business.
  • Digital content streaming platforms as they sale streaming of digital contents and it is a growing business on Amazon
  • Its database which helps it to keep records of its customers and they analyze the buying and browsing pattern of the customers and update them with the new offerings.
