We know that

(F/P,i,n)=(1+i)n

(F/P,2%,16)=(1+2%)16=1.372786

(F/P,2%,12)=(1+2%)12=1.268242

(F/P,2%,8)=(1+2%)8=1.171659

F=500*1.485947+500*1.372786+1000*1.268242+1000*1.171659=3869.27
