Product Costs:

Product costs are those costs which are directly attributable/ identified with the product. The following costs come under product costs.

1. Direct raw material costs

2. Direct labor costs

3. Manufacturing overhead costs.

Period Costs:

Period costs are those costs which are not directly attributable / identified with the product.

These costs are usually associated with selling and administrative activities of the business.
