Explanation:

Attorney's fees to organize the corporation is a start-up cost it's a capital expenditure.

Patent is an intangible asset. Purchase cost and other costs which are attributable to that purchase should be capitalized. Therefore purchase price of the patent and legal and other fees for transfer of the patent should be capitalized. Hence total value of the patent is $40,000 + $2,500 = $42,500 and entry is Debit the patent account $42,500 and Credit the cash account $ $42,500.
