WACC = cost of debt*(1-tax rare)*D/V + cost of equity*(E/V)

Here D/E = 0.5

        1+D/E = 1.5

         (E+D)/E = 1.5

           V/E =  1.5

            E/V = 1/1.5 = 0.67

           D/V = 0.33

So 9.5 = 8*(1-0.3)*0.33 + 0.67*cost of equity

 9.5 = 1.98 + 0.67* cost of equity

cost of equity = 11.45% 

Cost of equity will remain same even if debt to equity ratio changes.

new debt to equity = 0.8

D/E = 0.8

1+D/E = 1.8

V/E = 1.8

E/V = 1/1.8 = 0.56 times

D/V = 1-0.56 = 0.44

new WACC = 0.44*0.7*8 + 0.56*11.45
