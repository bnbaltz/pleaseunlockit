QUESTION:

Part of CH2M​ Hill's __________ is to move into as many new markets as possible by leveraging their expertise in environmental engineering. This direction was set in place by their Board of Directors.

 

A.

commitment

B.

environmental focus

C.

tactical plan

D.

​short-term plan

E.

strategic plan

ANSWER:

Strategic plans are designed  keeping broader picture of entire organization in mind aligned with vision and mission of the company. People who are involved in making strategic plans involve Board of Directors, CEO, CFO , President of divisions etc . They will design and execute strategic plans to paint a picture of the desired future and long-term goals of the organization. Essentially, strategic plans look ahead to where the organization wants to be in three, five, even ten years. Strategic plans, provided by top-level managers, serve as the framework for lower-level planning.

Tactical planning takes a company's strategic plan and sets forth specific short-term actions and plans, usually by company department or function. The tactical planning horizon is shorter than the strategic plan horizon

Thus Strategic plan at execution stage is broken into multiple tactical plans/ short term plans

Hill’s Plan of moving into as many markets as possible by leveraging their expertise in environmental engineering has following characteristics :

  1. A Strategic initiative in nature which leverages on company’s strength in environmental engineering
  2. The  plan is long term in nature since it wants to maximize number of markets which is possible only in the long run

Because of above reason , Hill’s plan is a “Strategic Plan”

HILL’S STRATEGIC PLAN IS TO MOVE INTO AS MANY MARKETS AS POSSIBLE BY LEVERAGING THEIR EXPERTISE IN ENVIRONMENTAL ENGINEERING
