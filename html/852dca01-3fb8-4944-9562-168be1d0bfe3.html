The multistep synthetic route for the compound A from benzaldehyde contains the following steps

1) The benzaldehyde is made to react with 1,3 -dithiol under acid catalyst, to form the thioacetal

2) This thioacetal has acidic proton, and is deprotonated by base, to convert the electrophilic carbon of aldehyde in to nucleophilic carbon (Umpolung or reversal of polarity).  Later it  reacts with chloro formic acid.

3) In the next step, the carbony protection was deprotected by reaction with mercuric chloride under aqueous condition to yield the alpha keto acid.

4) Since, grignard regent adds to more electrophilic keto group than the carboxylic group, followed by water work up under acidic condition to yield the desired product A.
