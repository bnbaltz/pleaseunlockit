Differential Cost:

Differential cost is the difference in choosing the two different alternatives. This cost is relevant in decision making.

Sunk Cost:

Sunk cost is the cost which is already incurred in the past and does not have any impact in future or current decision. It is the cost which is irrelevant in the decision making purpose.
