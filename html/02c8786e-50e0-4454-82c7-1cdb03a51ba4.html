SWOT ANALYSIS

Amazon's main strength is its advantage as the first in the market, access to resources, opportunities, networks that allow them to suppress competition. This power translates into greater market share and bargaining power of publishers, as well as a model warehouse that generates large sales volumes.

Amazon's weakness is its low presence of brick and mortar compared to the competition. Therefore, the buyer cannot become familiar with the first-hand product until he purchases it (Bossman 2010). This can affect the sale of Kindle online because buyers may have some concerns. The patented format of electronic books is also a weakness because there is a demand for more options and greater reach from customers.
