Electric Field


Initial set up: De-select Electric Field. Click on Values and Grid. And select Sensors from bottom. Drag a +1nC charge into the simulation area. 


1. Keep Sensor at a distance of 2 meter from charge. What is the value of electric field at
this point? Calculate the value by using formula of electric field. Do both values match?

2. As sensor is moved away from charge, how does the magnitude of electric field change?

3. Keep sensor at one point. Take +1nC more charge and place it on top of the +1nC
charge. How does the magnitude and direction of electric field change?

4. Can you predict the value of electric field at this point when five +1nC charges are stacked on top of each other?

5. Remove all positive charges. Let the sensor be at same point. Now take -1nC charge. What is the value of electric field? Do you observe any change?

6. Click on Electric Field. For a positive point charge, what is the pattern of electric field
lines?
A) Radially outward
B) Radially inwards
C) Along tangent

7. For a negative point charge, what is the pattern of electric field lines?
A) Radially outward
B) Radially inwards
C) Along tangent
