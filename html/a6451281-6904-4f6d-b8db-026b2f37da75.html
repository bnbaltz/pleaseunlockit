QUESTION:

A car drives at a constant speed on a circular, horizontal road. The net force on the car is zero. T/F?
A tangential force cannot change the direction of an objects velocity. T/F?
A small car and large truck collide head on. The magnitude of the resulting acceleration of the car is greater than that of the truck. T/F?

ANSWER:

(a) A car drives at a constant speed on a circular, horizontal road. The net force on the car is zero.   (FALSE)

(b) A tangential force cannot change the direction of an objects velocity.     (FALSE)

(c) A small car and large truck collide head on. The magnitude of resulting acceleration of the car is greater than that of the truck.    (TRUE)

Explanation : since the forces are same and F = ma, so the vehicle with lesser mass will have the greater acceleration. Cars are always faster on the road than trucks.
