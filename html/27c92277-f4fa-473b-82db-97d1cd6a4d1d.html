ANSWER:

Given:

1. The differential form "w" is called closed if dw = 0 

2. The differential form "w" is exact if there exist another differential form  such that w = d.

3. Suppose the open sets  are diffeomorphic (meaning that there is a differentiable that has a differential inverse  )

4. Suppose it is known that every closed form on U is exact 

To show : Every closed form on V is exact.

Solution: 

Since it is given that every closed form on U(U is diffeomorphic i.e. there is a differentiable that has a differential inverse  ) . is exact 

This implies there exist another differential form 1 such that U = d1.

Since U and V both are diffeomorphic.

Then differential form "V" is called closed if dV = 0

Also , this implies there exist another differential form 2 such that V = d2.

This implies every closed form on V is exact.

Hence we are done.
