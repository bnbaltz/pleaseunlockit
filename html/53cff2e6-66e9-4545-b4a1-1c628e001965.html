Sales Tax

Sales taxes are levied on retail transactions. It is collected by the merchant from customers and then it is forwarded to the respective government agency. Till such remittance happens, the collected amount is a current liability.

Prepare journal entry to record the sales and sales tax payable.

Explanation

• Accounts receivable is an asset account. When there is a sale made on account, the accounts receivable increases. Therefore, debit accounts receivable account by $3,472.

• Sales revenue account is a revenue account. It increases the value of equity. Therefore credit sales revenue account by $3,200.

• Sales tax payable is a liability and it increases by $272. Therefore, credit sales tax payable account by $272.
