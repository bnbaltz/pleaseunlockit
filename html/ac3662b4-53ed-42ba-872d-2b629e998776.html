ANSWER:

Matlab code:

t=0:0.001:1;
a1=10;
a2=3;    %amplitudes
a3=1;
f1=3;
f2=9;    %frequencies
f3=20+5;   %considered 3rd digit in id number as 5
p1=0;
p2=pi/4;    %phases
p3=pi/2;
x1=a1*sin((2*pi*f1*t)+p1);
x2=a2*sin((2*pi*f2*t)+p2);
x3=a3*sin((2*pi*f3*t)+p3);
x=x1+x2+x3;
figure(1)
plot(t,x1)
figure(2)
plot(t,x2)
figure(3)
plot(t,x3)
figure(4)
plot(t,x)  %plotting composite signal
