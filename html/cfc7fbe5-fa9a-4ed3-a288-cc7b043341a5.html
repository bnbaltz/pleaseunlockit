Question 1

Photodecomposition of AgCl takes place according to the following reaction;

2 AgCl ----------------> 2 Ag + Cl2 

Precipitate here is silver metal.

So, according to reaction, 

2 mol of AgCl gives = 2 mol of Ag

Molar mass of AgCl = 143.32 g/mol

Molar mass of Ag = 107.87 g/mol

So, 286.64 g of AgCl gives = 215.74 g of Ag

So, 1 g of AgCl gives = (215.74 / 286.64) g of Ag

10 mg of AgCl gives = 10 * (215.74 / 286.64) mg of Ag

10 mg of AgCl gives = 7.53 mg of Ag

Since 2 times decomposition takes place,

So, Total weight of the precipitate = 2 * 7.53 mg = 15.06 mg
