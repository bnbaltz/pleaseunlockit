QUESTION:

A 0.43kg pendulum bob is attached to a string 1.2 mlong.


What is the change in the gravitational potential energy of the system as the bob swings from point A to point B in the figure(Figure 1)

Change in U = ??? J

ANSWER:

The distance from the pivot straight down to the height of the pendulum bob is x 

cos 35 =x/1.2m 
1.2m *cos 35 = x 
x=0.983m 

1.2 -x 
1.2-0.983 
=0.217m height difference between A and B 

Ep=mgh 
= - (0.43 Kg)(9.81 m/s2)(0.217m) 
= - 0.915J since there is less gravitational energy at the lower point
