QUESTION:

A worker is tasked to work on a process requiring methanol during a 12-hour shift. The TLV ® for methanol is 200 ppm. What would be the adjusted TLV ® over the extended working period?

ANSWER:

  • Daily adjustment formula for TLV is given by
  • = TLV  8/h  (24-h)/16         h=no. of hours
  • = 200  8/12  (24-12)/16
  • = 100 ppm
  • The adjusted TLV has to be 100ppm for extended working period.
