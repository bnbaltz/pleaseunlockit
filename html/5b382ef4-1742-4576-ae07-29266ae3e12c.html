QUESTION:

Planning offers all of the following advantages EXCEPT:

 

  A.

A guarantee of success*

 
  B.

Helping us to sharpen our focus

 
  C.

Increasing our flexibility

 
  D.

Avoiding complacency

ANSWER:

A. A guarantee of success

Planning will help.in determine the road map and the approach that needs to be followed although success cannot be guaranteed as there are various other factors that contributes towards collective success as a whole.
