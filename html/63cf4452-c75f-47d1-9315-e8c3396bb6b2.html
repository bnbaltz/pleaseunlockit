QUESTION:

A battery is manufactured to have an emf of 24.0V, but the terminal voltage is only 22V when the battery is connected across a 7.5 Ohm Resistor. What is the internal resistance of the battery?

ANSWER:

 given
 emf of battery = E = 24v
  terminal voltage = V = 22v
 resistance R = 7.5Ω

 
   current   I = V/R
                  = 22V/7.5Ω
                  =2.93A
   from the equation E = V+Ir
                r = E-V/I
                   = 24v-22v/2.93A
                  = 0.68Ω
