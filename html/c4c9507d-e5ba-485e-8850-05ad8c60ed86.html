ATP SYNTHASE

The given figure shows electron transport chain in nner mitochondrial membrane.

During this process, electrons are transported from one electron carrier to the other. All the electron carriers are arranged in increasing order of their electrode potential. Ultimately, the electrons are accepted by oxygen to form water.

When electrons are transferred from one electron carrier to the other, protons are also pump to from mitochondrial matrix to intermembrane space. This results in the formation of a proton gradient across inner mitochondrial membrane. The protons are diffused back into the mitochondrial matrix through ATP synthase enzyme.

The electrochemical energy stored in proton gradient is utilised by ATP synthase enzyme for the synthesis of ATP. This is called as oxidative phosphorylation.
