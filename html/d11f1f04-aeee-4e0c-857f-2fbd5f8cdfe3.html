ANSWER:

1. Income statement equation

Sales - Variable expenses - Fixed expenses = Operating income

The breakeven sales is = Fixed expenses/ (Selling price - Variable cost)

= $1095000/ (12 - 4)

= 136875 cartons

2. 

Contribution margin ratio = Contribution/ Sales 

= $8/ 12 = 66.6666%

( Fixed expenses + Operating income )/ Contribution margin ratio = Target sales in dollars

The monthly sales needed to earn $312,000 in operating income is = ($1095000 + 312000) / 66.6666%

= $2110500

3. Contribution margin income statement

Sales (455000 x $12) $5460000
Less: Variable cost of goods sold (4 x 71% x 455000) -1292200
Gross contribution margin $4167800
Less: Variable operating expenses (4 x 29% x 455000) -527800
Net contribution margin $3640000
Less: Fixed expenses -1095000
Net operating income $2545000

4. 

Total sales - Breakeven sales = Margin of safety (in dollars)

The margin of safety is = $5460000 - ($12 x 136875) = $3817500

Contribution margin / Net operating income = Operating leverage factor

The operating leverage factor is = $3640000 / $2545000 = 1.430

5. If volume increases 11%, then operating income will increase = 11% x 1.430 = 15.73%
