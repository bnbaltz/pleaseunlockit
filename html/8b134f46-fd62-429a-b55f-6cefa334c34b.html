QUESTION:

Compared to Bt-susceptible bollworms, Bt-resistant individuals have longer development times on normal cotton but higher survival rates on Bt cotton, meaning there is a trade-off between development time and survival. Knowing this, what would the most likely consequence of the evolution of Bt-resistant without longer development time? 

a. resistance could not evolve in any population

b. Bt-toxin would kill every bollworm, and the entire species would become extinct

c. Resistance would evolve more quickly because it would have no associated fitness cost 

d. there would be no consequence, the trade-off does not affect the evolution of resistance 

ANSWER:

Bt resistant bollworms have longer developmental time on normal cotton.

It have higher survival rate on bt cotton.

So reason for evolution of bt resistance without longer developmental time may be

Resistance would evolve more quickly because it would have no associated fitness cost.
