There is circular loop magnetic filed is perpendicularly downward

When ‘B’ is increasing, lines of force going downward increases, hence current in coil flows such that lines of force due to induced current in coil are going upward. Hence the direction of current in loop is anticlockwise

When ‘B’ decreases, lines of magnetic force going downward decreases, hence now induced current in coil is such that magnetic lines of force of coil are also going downward. Hence current in coil is clockwise

When ‘B’ is constant, there is no induced current in coil.
