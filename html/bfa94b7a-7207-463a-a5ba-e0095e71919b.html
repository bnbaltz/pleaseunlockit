ANSWER:

Entropy of the system = Entropy of the products - Entropy of the reactants

Thus, Ssystem = [ 205 + 2*69.940] -[2*110] J/K

Ssystem = 344.88 - 220 J/K

Ssystem = 124.8 J/K

Entropy of surrounding = - Enthalpy of system / Temperature

Since, temprature is not specified, we will consider it is 298K.

Now, 

Enthalpy of the system = Enthalpy of the products - Enthalpy of the reactants 

Thus, Hsystem = [ 2*-285.84] -[2*-187.8] J

Hsystem = -196.08 kJ

Ssurrounding = - Hsystem /T

= 196.08 kJ / 298 K

=657.98 J/K

Entropy of universe = Entropy of system + Entropy of surrounding

Suniverse = Ssystem + Ssurrounding

Suniverse = 124.8 + 657.98 = 782.78 J/K
