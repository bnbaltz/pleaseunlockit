The correct answer would be b (E).
Progesterone is a hormone that supports pregnancy by increasing blood circulation between the uterus and and the placenta and also stimulates the growth of mammary glands for milk production. Hence, during the early phase of pregnancy, the levels keep on increasing, and remains high throughout the pregnancy term.
