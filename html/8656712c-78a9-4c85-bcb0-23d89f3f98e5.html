QUESTION:

Name other scientific revolutions that happened in the following places:

a.Mesoamerica

b. asia

c. middle east

d. africa 

ANSWER:

                  The answer to this question is:-

 

 a Mesoamerica - They brought up extremely durable blue paint, Which was used in Mayan art. This paint was unkown in any part of the world. Also notable observations in heart surgery. They believed in cycles of planets and celestial bodies like the sun, and the moon. Also they built up a observatory.

 

b Asia- Asians were the first to introduce paper to the world. Also silk was a great scientific development. Ceramics were used in domestic wares and utensils. Indians were the first to introduce decimal system and zero number. Also notable achievements have been made in astronomy during the period of Aryabhatta.

 

c Middle east-     Their scientific revolution is well known for chemical  sulfuric acid, as well as many other chemicals and instruments. Developed theorems,  roots using the decimal system and algebra, Arabic numerals and algorithms. 

 

d Africa:-  They have oldest stone tools in the world. They used 3 types of calendar-Lunar calendar, Solar calendar, stellar calendar. Egyptian are known for human anatomy- mummification. First tillage tools used to establish crops were by Africans.
