Investment

The process of sacrificing today’s money for a future return is known as investment.

Trading Securities

Trading investments are the equity shares or debt securities where the investor holds less than 20% of the voting stocks.

Unrealized holding gain/loss

Unrealized holding gain/loss is a gain/loss that results from holding on an investment. It does not realize the profit/loss in the form of actual cash.
