N7gC-oY5 Company prepared the following aging schedule for its accounts

The net realizable value of N7gC-oY5 Company's accounts receivable at

Calculate the amount of accounts receivable that were 51-100 days past
due.

Net realizable value of accounts receivable = Accounts receivable- Allowance for uncollectible accounts

618,710 = (590,900+ K) -  (123,084+ 0.17K)

618,710 = 590,900+K- 123,084-0.17K

618,710 = 467,816+0.83K
