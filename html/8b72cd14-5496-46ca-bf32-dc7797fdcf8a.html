When a capital asset is purchased, you have to consider following costs as part of the cost of capital assets-

1. actual cost of capital assets

2. All relevant costs incurred for its acquisition. Example, transportation, brokerage, loading unloadinh cost etc.

3. All costs necessary to make it ready for use.

 In this problem, Land has been purchased at a cost of $600,000. associated costs are brokers commission of $40,000, title Insurance $3,000 and miscellaneous closing cost of $8,000. as all these costs are essential to procure the land, they are included in the cost.
