QUESTION:

At the end of the year, Johan Company has $50,000 in wages due to its employees on January 4th. Where would adjustments be made?

 

Select an answer:

  • in Prepaid Expenses

  • in Unearned Revenues

  • in Unrecorded Liabilities

  • in Unrecorded Receivables

ANSWER:

Employees help in generating revenue.

The wages that are paid to them are matched to the revenue that they genrate according to the matching principle.

The wages are not yet been paid, so it is a libility to Johan company.

It is an unrecorded liability and can be shown as wages payable

The correct answer is unrecorded liability
