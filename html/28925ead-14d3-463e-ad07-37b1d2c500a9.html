Product costs:

Product costs also called as manufacturing costs or inventoriable costs. Product costs include all the costs that are involved in manufacturing a product. These costs are assigned to inventory account to be reported in the balance sheet.

Period costs:

Period costs are the costs which are not included in product costs. These costs are reported in the income statement as and when they are incurred. Hence these costs are not included in cost of purchased or manufactured goods. All selling and administrative costs will form part of period costs.
