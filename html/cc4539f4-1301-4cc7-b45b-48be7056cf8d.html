QUESTION:

  • identify and label the following in each electron micrograph:

–the mRNA transcript

–the ribosomes

–the newly synthesized protein 

  • Can you find the DNA encoding the newly synthesized protein? Label the DNA.
  • Label the 5’ and 3’ ends of the DNA and RNA molecules

ANSWER:

 in prokaryotes, as both replication as well as protein synthesis occurs in the cytoplasm, therefore transcription and translation occurs simultaneously. Therefore both DNA and mRNA are visible.

However in eukaryotes, Transcription occurs in nucleus and protein synthesis occurs in cytoplasm, therefore only RNA attached to ribosomes is seen.
