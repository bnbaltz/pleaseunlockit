QUESTION:

What are some important differences between new venture business plans and business plans of established firms?

ANSWER:

Aner:

There are lot of aspects in which business plan differ but some main ones are as follows

1. Strategy : This is an important part for any buiness . for an established business this part has already been done and their are high chance that choosen strategy has turned out to be effective. on the other hand, for a new venture they are at  stage where their strategy is based on a lot of ifs and buts. They may have done a lot of market research and all but the result in totality is yet to be seen. They must have a contingency strategy ready in case the current strategy fails to deliver 

2.Market scenario: Existing buiness know the market, they understand the demand, supply scenario for their products. on the other hand, start ups have to carefully study the market, make lot of assumptions in understanding the market requirements to determine demand for their product

Legal: Existing buinesses have legal team in plsace to counter in case the failure happens where new business venture have to be very cautious and take this contigency in to consideration  as well and be prepared for it.if any of their product faces the heat of the cutomer or activists

Brand effect: establsihed buiness strategy have a benfit of brand value recognition where as new business venture buiness plans have to carve out their space in the existing market and deliver good quality product with a focused marketing to build customer recognition
