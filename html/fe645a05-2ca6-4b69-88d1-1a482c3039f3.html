Business Ideas:

  1. Staring a farmer’s market from where other produce like vegetables, fruits, fish, milk and milk products might be sold. It can also be used to sell furniture built at the farm.
  2. Advertise the farm as a destination for parties and weddings. The new barn is a very good space for parties. As there are metropolitan areas nearby, customers might like the peace and serenity of the surroundings to have their weddings or other events.
  3. Bed and Breakfast. The farm can be advertised as a bed and breakfast for the city dwellers nearby. Customers can spend the weekend at the farm, partake in farm activities and enjoy the healthy food. It can be promoted as a green vacation as the farm uses green energy.
  4. Set up a small technical institute. Use the skills of Earl and Teev to train local youth. This will help in creating a steady flow of income, build a stronger bond with the local community and generate captive talent who can support Earl and Teev in operating the farm.
  5. Building a distillery on the farm. Some portion of the land can be devoted to growing barley which can then be used for the production of beer. This is a little capital intensive but will bring in a number of visitors and a scenic tavern could be opened on the farm.
