QUESTION:

Determine the magnitude of the magnetic flux through the south-facing window of a house in British Columbia, where Earth's B⃗ field has a magnitude of 5.8 × 10−5 T and the direction of B⃗ field is 72∘ below horizontal with the horizontal component of B⃗ field directed to the north. Assume the area of the window is 2.0 m2 . Ans is 3.6 x 10^-5

 

Part B Determine the magnitude of the magnetic flux through the west-facing window that has the same area.

ANSWER:

A)

Flux, phi = BA cos(theta)

Phi = 5.8 x 10^-5 x 2 x cos(72) = - 3.6 x 10^-5 Wb

B)

Flux, phi = BA cos(theta)

Phi = 5.8 x 10^-5 x 2 x cos(90) = 0 Wb
