QUESTION:

Identify each of the following as a strong acid, weak acid, strong base, or weak base

HC7H5O2

LiOH

HClO4

NaOH

HNO2

CH3NH2

 

ANSWER:

HC7H5O2      ------ weak acid

because its Conjugate Acid is HC7H5O2, a weak acid

     C7H5O2-(aq)+ H2O(l) <------> HC7H5O2(aq)+ OH-(aq)

LiOH            ------ weak base

HClO4          -----  strong acid

NaOH          ----- strong base

HNO2          ----- weak base

CH3NH2     ------ weak base
