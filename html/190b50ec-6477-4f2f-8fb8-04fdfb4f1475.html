ANSWER:

Q1) The answer is (d) marginal product  of 4th worker = total product with 4 workers - total product with 3 workers

= 16 - 12 = 4 sloths

Q2) The answer is (c) as if the marginal product cuts average product when it is at its maximum from top. Thus, if MP < AP, the average product must be decreasing

Q3) The answer is (d) as if total output is increasing but not as much, there are diminishing returns to labor and the marginal product is thus positive but decreasing

Q4) The answer is (d) as total product is increasing as long as MP is increasing and will reach the maximum point when MP = 0 which happens when quantity is Q4
