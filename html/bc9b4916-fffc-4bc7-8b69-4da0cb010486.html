QUESTION:

Fifth Fourth National Bank has a savings program which will guarantee you $16,500 in 15 years if you deposit $65 per month. What APR is the bank offering you on this savings plan?

ANSWER:

Answer:

Given 

Future Value FV=$16500

Period  N=15*12=180 months

Payment per month A=$65

Let r be monthly interest rate 

So FV=A*((1+r)^N-1)/r

16500=65*(1-(1+r)^-180)/r

So solving for r we get r= 0.36%

So APR=12*r=12*0.36%=4.38%
