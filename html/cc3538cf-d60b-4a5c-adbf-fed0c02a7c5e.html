ANSWER:

Mass of block A (mA) = 5 kg

Mass of block B (mB) = 15 kg

Acceleration of box B (a) = 5.8 m/s2

Acceleration of box A = acceleration of box B = a = 5.8 m/s2

Let the tension in the string be T

 

Force on blobk B in the downward direction = mBg

Force on block B in the upward direction = T

Net force on the box B = (mBg - T)

This force causes an acceleration 'a' of the box B

=> mBg - T = mBa

=> T = mB(g - a)

plugging the values we get

T = 15*(9.8 - 5.8) = 60 N

 

Force on box B towards right = T

Let the force of friction acting on box A be Ff

This force of friction acts on box A towards left

Net force on box A = (T - Ff)

This force causes acceleration 'a' of box A

=> T - Ff = mA*a

=> Ff = T - mA*a

plugging the values we get

Ff = 60 - 5*5.8

=> Ff = 31 N

 

Hence the size of the force of friction acting on box A is 31N (2nd option)
