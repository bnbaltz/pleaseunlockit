QUESTION:

Tows matrix and strategies selection to solve main problems and issues for Amazon construct a TWOS Matrix and describe the most relevant strategies for the firm to solve its main problems and challenges.

ANSWER:

Answer : 

 

TWOS stands for Threats Weaknesses Opportunities and Strengths or commonly known as swot analysis. This is used to identify the key strengths of a firm and where it needs to improve. This also offers an through assessment of the competitive landscape by identifying threats. 

 

  Weaknesses Strength
Threats Threats and Weaknesses : Wallmart and other retail players who are turning into e commerce players. Amazon does not have a dominant foothold in south east asian countries Threats and Strengths : Increasing logistics and shipping costs. However amazon is strengthened by its vast warehouses and supply chain optimisation.
Opportunities Opportunities and Weaknesses : Developing economies have a large base of middle class customers and amazon has a great opportunity awaiting for it right there. Its weak in the south east asian markets due to regulatory and scalability issues. Opportunities and Strengths : Amazon could also improve in vertical ecommerce rather than purely horizontal ecommerce. By focusing on certain product verticles it could specilise in niche products and increase it sales. Amazon's greatest strength lies in it diversified services. As of now Amazon has its ecommerce. alexa, kindle, AWS or amazon webservices. amazon prime streaming, its own payment gateway and logistics support.
