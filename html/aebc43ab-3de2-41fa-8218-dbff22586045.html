3NF Relational Schema

EDITOR(EditorID, EditorLName, EditorFName, EditorInstitution)

EDITS(EditorID, BookNbr, EditorOrder)

BOOK(BookNbr, BookISBN, BookTitle, BookPrice, VolumeBookNbr)

INCLUDES(BookNbr,ChapterID)

CHAPTER(ChapterID, ChapterNumber, ChapterTitle)

AUTHORASSIGNMENT(AuthorPosition, AuthorType, ChapterID, AuthorID)

AUTHOR(AuthorID, AuthLastName, AuthFirstName, AuthMiddleInitial, AuthPhone, AuthEmail)

AUTHOREXP(AuthorID,AuthorExpertise)

ORDERLINE(OrderlineNbr, OLPrice,OLDiscount,OLQuantity, OrderID, BookNbr)

ORDER(OrderID, OrderDate, OrderDeliveryDate, CustID)

WHOLESALER(CustID, CustName)

underlined are primary keys and italicized are foreign keys. Some attributes are both.
