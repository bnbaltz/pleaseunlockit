QUESTION:

create a TOWS matrix for Amazon Consider the effect of globalization when identifying corporate strategies.

ANSWER:

Amazon is a world-famous online shopping brand. Globalization leads to a better scope for Amazon and improves efficiency, coordination, and performance. With the help of TOWS analysis, Amazon summarizes its threats, opportunities, weaknesses, and strengths, based on globalization aspects. 
Threats: because of globalization, Amazon is continuously facing a competitive market worldwide. Also, the company is entirely dependent on venders. It indicates the company is still not aware of supplier opportunities due to globalization. Patent violation disputes are the most significant threats for Amazon nowadays as the public become aware of patents through improved digitization techniques. There is constant pressure on Amazon to sell environmentally friendly products in the market.
Opportunities: globalization increases e-commerce opportunities as people are more comfortable to buy products from their homes. Growth in internet usage in China, India, and the US can increase the sales of the company. Growth in digital media and social media can be used as a selling platform by the company.
Weaknesses: instead of an increasing number of internet usage in China, Amazon performs very low in this country. In this era of the globalization era, weak cash flow must be considered as a risk factor for Amazon. Amazon does not sell products in the low-profit margin like Wal-Mart. This is why Amazon cannot stand first in the global market instead of having the widest variety of products. 
Strengths: Amazon is known for its strong brand value worldwide. Versatility, influential research, and development division and strong technological application are the key factors for the success of this company. Amazon holds the most skilled workforce. It values customers' demands and choices.
