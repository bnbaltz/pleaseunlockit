QUESTION:

A random sample of 4 homes for sale in NW Gainesville between 16000 and 20000 square feet had a mean of $156100 and a standard deviation of $13660. There are no outliers in the sample data set. Construct a 95% confidence interval for the average home of this size.

(142713.20, 169486.80)
(141538.2, 170.661.56)
(134366.94,177833.06)
(144864.65, 167335.35)

ANSWER:

Solution :

Given that,

Point estimate = sample mean =  = $156100

Population standard deviation =  = $13660

Sample size = n = 4

 At 95% confidence level the z is ,

  = 1 - 95% = 1 - 0.95 = 0.05

  / 2 = 0.05 / 2 = 0.025

  Z/2 = Z 0.025 = 1.96

Margin of error = E = Z/2* ( /n) 

= 1.96 * (13660 / 4) 

= 13386.8

At 95% confidence interval estimate of the population mean is,

 - E <  <  + E

156100 - 13386.8 <  < 156100 + 13386.8

142713.20 <  < 169486.80

(142713.20 , 169486.80 )

A 95% confidence interval for the average: (142713.20 , 169486.80)
