QUESTION:

A particularly large insect has a body that is segmented into three main parts. The first part is 2 g and is located 1 cm from the end of the body, the second part is 5 g and is located 4 cm from the end, and the third part is 3 g and is located 9 cm from the end. If the end is considered as the origin, where is the center of mass of the insect? Consider each segment as a point particle.

ANSWER:

4.9 cm from the end
