QUESTION:

A 4-A Current is maintained in a simple circuit with a total resistance of 2 Ohms. How much energy is delivered if forty five seconds? note: please give as much explination as possible. Thank you!
a)1440 J
b)96 J
C)360 J
d)720 J
e)240 J

ANSWER:

Current i = 4 A
 
Resistance R = 2 ohm
 
time t = 45 s

 
Energy delivered Q = i^2*R*t
 
                             = (4)^2*2*45
 
                             = 16*2*45
 
                             = 1440J
 
So option (a) is correct
