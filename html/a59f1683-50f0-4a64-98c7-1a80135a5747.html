QUESTION:

Part of CH2M​ Hill's work includes allowing specific employees to be responsible for finding and developing new projects. A SWOT analysis would identify these new projects as​ a(n) ________.

A.

strength

B.

opportunity

C.

strategy

D.

weakness

E.

threat

ANSWER:

SWOT analysis talks about Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities and Threats of a business/organisation when it operates in market. This analysis helps a company to know various aspects of its business in terms of what are its strengths, weaknesses, what all opportunities lie in the market in which it is operating in terms of new product and service etc and what are the potential threats from competitors iin terms of quality , material etc .

In CH2M​ Hill's work,employees have been asked to find and develop new projects. This means they have been taked to find new opportunities for the company.Thus, a SWOT analysis will identify these projects as OPPORTUNITY which is option B.
