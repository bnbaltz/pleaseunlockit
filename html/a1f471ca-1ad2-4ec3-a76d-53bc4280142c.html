Question 9

when you Provide services to the customers on account, the journal entry is debit Accounts Receivable and credit sales revenue. Accounts receivable is part of current assets and increases the assets. Sales revenue after considering the expenses goes to retained earnings which increases the Equity

Hence option c) Increase assets and d) Increase equity are correct

Question 10

Journal entry for Purchase of business cards on account is to debit the expense on purchase of cards and credit the accounts payable account. The business card expenses decreases the retained earnings and thus decreases the equity which is part of it. Accounts payable comes under current liabilities which increases liabilities

Hence option c) Increase liabilities and d) decrease equity are correct
