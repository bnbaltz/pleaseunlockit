ANSWER:

Coal is formed from the burial of plant matter. The heat and pressure within the interior of the Earth causes the conversion of this plant matter into coal and petroleum i.e. the coal is formed from the geothermal energy from within the Earth. This geothermal energy is stored within the coal, and when the gasolene is burnt, this energy is released to power the car. 

Thus, the primary source of energy in this case is the geothermal energy from within the Earth.
