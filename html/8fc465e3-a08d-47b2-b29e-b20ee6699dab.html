QUESTION:


ANSWER:

Answer: A gain of $5,000

 

Explanation:

 

Gain / (Loss) on sale of Equipment = Sale price - Book value

 

Cost of the Equipment (Given)= $100,000

Accumulated Depreciation (Given)= $65,000

Book value of Equipment = Cost - Accumulated Depreciation

                                         = 100,000 - 65,000

                                         = $35,000

Sale price of Equipment (Given)= $40,000

 

Gain/(Loss) on sale of Equipment = Sale price of Equipment - Book value of Equipment

                                                          = 40,000 - 35,000

                                                          = $5,000 

Since sale price ($40,000) is greater than Book value ($35,000), company will recognise a gain of $5,000 on sale of equipment.
