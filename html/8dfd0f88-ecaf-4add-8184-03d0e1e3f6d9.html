QUESTION:

The company where you are employed wishes to produce latex gloves in a more profitable manner. You have been assigned to conduct a comparison study in order to assist the company in making a decision. The latex gloves can be produced using three different alternatives; Alternative K1, Alternative K2 or Alternative K3. With each alternative, the estimated capital investment amount as well as its annual expenses savings are given in Table S3. For Alternative K1, the annual savings are estimated to increase at the rate of 6% per year (Hint: f = 6%). Annual savings for Alternative K2 are estimated to increase at a constant rate of RM200 per year starting after year one (Hint: G=RM200). For the Alternative 3, the savings are of uniform series. Assume that the MARR is 12% per year with a useful life of each alternative is six years. Market value at the end of year six is zero for all three alternatives.

a)Determine the order of the alternatives priority and explain its basis of order.

b)Is the selected base alternative acceptable? Show how did you come up with that conclusion.

c)Determine the PW for all alternatives.

d)Using the incremental analysis method, determine the preferred alternative among all three given alternatives.

ANSWER:

It is given that useful life of each alternative is 6 years and market value of each alternative at end of 6 years is 0

using the present worth analysis, alternative K1 is preferred as it has higher present worth 

now figuring out which alternative is preferred using the incremental analysis

out of the 3 alternatives, K3 has the lowest initial investment

taking K3 as defender and K1 as challenger (with higher initial investment)

incremental rate of return of alternative K1 over alterntaive  K3 is 66.266% which is greater than MARR of 12%

Therefore, alternative K1 is preferred over alternative K3

now comparing K1 with K2

taking K1 as defender and K2 as challenger

alternative k1 is preferred over alternative k2 

alterntive K1 is preferred over other alternatives
